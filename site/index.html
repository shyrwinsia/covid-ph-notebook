Philippine COVID-19 Cases

Jan 30, 2020 - Present

This is a choropleth map that shows the relative COVID-19 cases between municipalities and cities of the Philippines. The cases are normalized with the population of each local government unit. This provides an objective comparison of cases across boundaries, taking into account relative population densities.

This map aims to make patterns visible and provide insights on the spread of the disease and the effects of the community and government efforts. This only provides a "big picture" and does not have any interpretation of the data. This is left to you, the viewer.

All the data is free and comes from the public domain. The accuracy of the data will be done on best-effort policy. I will not be held liable for any damages that result in the usage of this map. All data is entered manually and that means this map may not be always up to date.
